* **The Sovereign Loop:** Government buys satellite truth $\rightarrow$ That funding builds global infrastructure.
* **The Grassroots Loop:** Farmers and Indigenous groups get free tools $\rightarrow$ They build the regeneration economy while the extraction economy unknowingly pays for it.

* **Within ten years:** The extraction debt becomes conservation funding, the stolen land becomes native‑managed, and the story becomes reality.
